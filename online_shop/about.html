About AMS

Welcome to AMS, your number one source for all things shopping. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of products, with a focus on quality, customer service, and uniqueness.

Founded in 2025 by Jane Smith, AMS has come a long way from its beginnings in a home office. When Jane first started out, her passion for helping customers find the best products drove her to do intense research, quit her day job, and gave her the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming online store.

We now serve customers all over the world and are thrilled to be a part of the fair trade wing of the retail industry.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
